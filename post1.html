How to Improve Your Credit Score in 2025

Published on July 31, 2025 | Author : Akshay

Your credit score plays a vital role in determining your financial credibility. In 2025, lenders are more data-driven than ever. Here's how to stay ahead.

Pay Your Bills on Time

Payment history is the most significant factor in your credit score. Automate payments to never miss a due date.

Reduce Credit Card Balances

Keep your credit utilization ratio below 30%. This shows lenders you’re not over-reliant on credit.

Don’t Close Old Accounts

Older accounts show a long credit history, which benefits your score even if they're unused.
